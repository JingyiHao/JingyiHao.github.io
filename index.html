Shanghai Disney Resort Introduction

Shanghai Disneyland, located at 310 Huang Zhao Road, Chuansha Town, Pudong New Area, Shanghai, officially opened on June 16, 2016, is the first Disney theme park in mainland China and one of the largest Sino-foreign cooperation projects in China's modern service industry, a theme park with pure Disney style and a blend of Chinese style.

Shanghai Disneyland covers an area of 1.16 square kilometers, the theme park is divided into Mickey Street, Garden of Wonder, Adventure Island, Treasure Bay, Tomorrowland, Fantasyland, Disney-Pixar Toy Story. With Disney Castle, Marvel Heroes Headquarters, Buzz Lightyear Star Rescue and other amusement projects. 2020 visitor volume reached 5.5 million.

In 2017, Shanghai Disney Resort, including Shanghai Disneyland, won the "2016-2017 China Construction Engineering Luban Award". On March 24, 2020, the 11th China Best Cultural Tourism Awards were released, and Shanghai Disneyland won the "Best Cultural Tourism Award of the Year". As of October 7, 2022, the total number of domestic and international visitors has reached 103.34 million.

Main Attractions

  • Dream World

Fantasy World is located in the north of Shanghai Disneyland, there are 9 amusement projects, respectively, fantasy fairy tale castle, Alice in Wonderland maze, crystal colorful voyage, roaming fairy tale time, seven dwarfs mine car, Peter Pan sky adventure, Winnie the Pooh adventure, rotating crazy honey pot, welcome pavilion selfie spot.

  • Tomorrow's World

Tomorrowland is located in the northwestern part of Shanghai Disneyland, with a total of five amusement projects, namely Buzz Lightyear Intergalactic Rescue, Genesis Aurora Wheel - Chevrolet Presents, Genesis: Chevrolet Digital Challenge, Jetpack Flight, and Space Luck Stitch.

  • Whimsical Garden

Fantasy Garden is located in the northern part of Shanghai Disneyland, with four amusement projects, namely Fantasia Carousel, Marvel Heroes Headquarters, Dumbo, and Fantasy Garden Selfie Spot.

  • Treasure Bay

Treasure Bay is located in the northeast of Shanghai Disneyland, a total of four play projects, respectively, boat strange play beach, Pirates of the Caribbean - the battle of sunken treasure, the mystery of the Siren's Revenge, explorer canoe.

  • Expedition Island

Adventure Island is located in the southeast of Shanghai Disneyland, a total of four play programs, respectively, soaring - leap horizon, laughter gathering friends self-timer point, Thunder Mountain rafting, monuments to explore the camp.

  • Disney-Pixar Toy Story

Disney-Pixar Toy Story is located in the northwest of Shanghai Disneyland, there are 3 amusement projects, respectively, Cuddle Dragon Sky Race, Spring Dog Cluster, and Woody Cowboy Carnival.

Opening hours

Shanghai Disneyland is open daily from 9:00 am to 20:30 pm or 10:00 am to 20:30 pm. All park hours and information are for reference only and are subject to change on or before the day you visit the park. Please visit Guest Services on the day of your visit to obtain a park schedule for the most up-to-date details of the day's activities and changes.
